## The objective (user, 2026-07-17)
The reduced format makes the ENTIRE grid four arrays -- V, I_bus, Icomp, Y -- and every
task is a visibility pattern over them (pf/se/injection/param are 4 examples of many;
there is no P/Q or anything else in this representation). THE objective: mask any subset
of the four, reconstruct the rest -- under bad/noisy visible entries too.
"Any subset" includes arbitrary scalar/slot masks, not only whole arrays or components;
the graph topology is provided and must transfer zero-shot; missing-edge inference is a
separate later capability and is not conflated with the current completion metric.
Status: all four arrays are now maskable as whole arrays, components, slots, or scalar
real/imag entries; learned unseen accuracy is pending. Y remains excitation-limited per T22.
